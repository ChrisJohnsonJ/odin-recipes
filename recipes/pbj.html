Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich Recipe

Description

A basic and simple recipe. Good for an easy breakfast. Serve with a hot cup of coffee

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly(any flavour)
  • Butter
  • Banana

Preparation

  1. Heat the pan and apply a dab of butter.
  2. Toast the bread with butter.
  3. Spread the peanut butter on side of a toast and jelly on the another toast
  4. Cut bananas and fruits for the sandwich.
  5. Sprinkle some sugar dust on the sandwich if necessary.
